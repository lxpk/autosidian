# Code of conduct

We want contribution and discussion around **Autosidian** and the **Autosidia** registry to stay constructive and safe for everyone.

- **Be respectful** — Disagree on ideas, not people. No harassment, discrimination, or hate.
- **Assume good faith** — Especially in async text; ask for clarity before conflict.
- **Keep scope in mind** — Link to [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) for security-sensitive reports instead of public threads.

For enforcement and escalation, maintainers will follow a lightweight process: warning for minor issues, removal of access for repeated or severe violations. The exact maintainer list and contact path will be published when the project has a public home.

To **contribute** or report problems with the work itself, see [CONTRIBUTING.md](CONTRIBUTING.md) and [SUPPORT.md](SUPPORT.md). For **vulnerabilities**, use [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
